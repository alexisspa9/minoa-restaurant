Our Restaurant

* * *

MINOA restaurant in Naoussa Paros is Paros one of the oldest traditional restaurant on the island. It has been operating since 1977 when there were few shops in the alleys of the village. It is a reference point for those who love good, traditional Greek cuisine and a warm, friendly environment to enjoy their meal. It is a family restaurant located on a quiet side in the center of Naoussa that has been operating for three generations.

In our cooking we use fresh ingredients offered by the Parian land as well as the highest quality Greek products. We will be very happy to offer you our quality traditional flavors in our calm, renewed and comfortable space environment.
